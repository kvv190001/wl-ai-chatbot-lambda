1. What is an enterprise AI chatbot?
- An enterprise AI chatbot is an intelligent conversational interface that uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to understand and respond to business-related questions. WorldLink's Assistant Chatbot leverages pgvector for semantic search, Mixtral for language understanding, and Azure cloud infrastructure to deliver secure, scalable, and context-aware responses for both customer-facing and internal employee use cases.

3. What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)?
- RAG is an advanced AI technique that enhances chatbot responses by retrieving relevant information from your organization's document repository before generating answers. When a user asks a question, the system searches your knowledge base using pgvector's semantic search capabilities, retrieves the most relevant documents, and uses that context to generate accurate, source-backed responses. This ensures the chatbot provides answers grounded in your actual business data rather than generic information.

13. What are state machines and why are they important?
- State machines are a design pattern that manages complex conversation flows by defining distinct states and transitions between them. In our chatbot, state machines handle multi-step processes like authentication, form filling, escalation workflows, and context switching. This ensures conversations remain coherent, the chatbot remembers context across multiple messages, users can navigate complex processes smoothly, and the system handles edge cases and errors gracefully. State machines make the chatbot feel intelligent and conversational rather than rigid and scripted.

15. What is pgvector and why is it used?
- pgvector is a PostgreSQL extension that enables high-performance vector similarity search, which is essential for RAG implementations. When documents are added to the chatbot's knowledge base, they are converted into mathematical vectors (embeddings) that capture semantic meaning. When a user asks a question, pgvector quickly finds the most semantically similar documents, even if they don't share exact keywords. This enables the chatbot to understand intent and retrieve relevant information with remarkable accuracy and speed.

16. What is Mixtral and why was it chosen?
- Mixtral is a state-of-the-art open-source large language model developed by Mistral AI. We chose Mixtral for its exceptional performance on business and technical tasks, strong multilingual capabilities, efficient inference speed enabling real-time responses, ability to follow complex instructions accurately, and cost-effectiveness compared to proprietary models. Mixtral provides enterprise-grade language understanding while allowing deployment in your own infrastructure, ensuring data privacy and control.
